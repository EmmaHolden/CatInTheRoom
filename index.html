Cat in the Room

Your task is to find the cat inside this room.

Sounds easy? Well this isn't just your ordinary cat.

This is a magical cat with powers of invisibility.

Whenever you click the mouse, the invisible cat will meow.

The volume of the meow will tell you how close or far away you are.

If you can't play with sound, press spacebar during the game for silent mode.

So turn your speakers up and press start to begin!
